Priority 1

Life Safety Issues

  • Fire, electrical sparking, gas leak
  • Person trapped (elevator/room)
  • Structural collapse or danger
  • Hazardous material spill

Critical Facilities Issues

  • Major flooding (active)
  • No water (building-wide)
  • No heat (winter <0°C)
  • Sewage backup (occupied areas)
  • Exposed electrical wiring
  • Secure door won't lock (after-hours)
  • Ice on main walkways

⬆️ Escalate Priority If:

  • Multiple reports of same issue
  • Exam or event affected
  • Issue worsening rapidly
  • Accessibility barrier created
  • After-hours with AM impact
